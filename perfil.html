Who Am I?

Hi I'm Patrick Morais, nice to meet you!

I am currently a Software Quality Engineer at PagSeguro UOL, I am a computer scientist certified in software testing, and my previous experiences in the industry, internships and startups have included collaboration at different stages of the software development process, including business analysis, data management, product and team leadership.

In addition, to being familiar with the software development and quality assurance process (Web, Mobile, APIs), I also had the opportunity to take courses in the area of ​​continuous integration and agile methodologies and could apply them over some projects.

I am passionate about social entrepreneurship and volunteer work, taking part in Latin America's largest entrepreneurship fairs and courses and also founding a cultural association that has allowed me to directly impact more than 700 young people through culture-related activities.

I've been leading some projects, one of them being nationally prominent in the area of ​​urban mobility.

I recently won a scholarship at Tecnológico de Monterrey - Mexico (5th Best University of Technology in Latin America), where I studied leadership and technology with over 50 young people from around the world.

After my exchange in Mexico, I had the opportunity to go through a full immersion in Silicon Valley - California where I met several people, companies, incubators, investors, universities and designed the next steps of my career.

This is a piece of my story, if you want to know more or discuss anything cool, please drop me a message!
