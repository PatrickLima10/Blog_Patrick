About

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Patrick Morais

I was born in Goiânia and I currently live in São Paulo. I do Computer Science under a full scholarship from PROUNI at Anhembi Morumbi and I am a Software Quality Engineer.

Well, I am proud of my story that began to change when I got a scholarship to study at the college of the military police of Goiás. Through this college I met a group of people who introduced me to the CAJU (Youth House) that had the objective of fighting for basic rights of children and adolescents of the popular milieu, I entered the CAJU and began to help in the projects and interventions that were made. At the age of 15, I left the CAJU and founded a cultural association that aimed to bring culture to people from the outlying regions of Goiânia. I presided over the project for 2 years and during my management there were more than 700 people directly impacted with the workshops that were made. Throughout this period I took my livelihood from internships in companies that helped me a lot to grow.

Already in college I awoke to social entrepreneurship and founded Systems Way, a startup focused on technology and innovation for the area of ​​urban mobility. Through Systems Way I participated in the biggest fairs, courses and events of entrepreneurship and technology in the world, one of them the Campus party where I was champion of the pitch battle and I was also the first Goiano selected for the Future Campus that is a project that has as mentoring and visibility for social impact projects.

Already in 2018, working in one of the largest software companies in Goiás - Brazil and dividing my time between English, university and work classes, I was one of the 41 selected (more than 6,000 enrolled) for ProLíder, the largest training program of young leaders in Brazil. In ProLíder we basically went through a training with renowned people where diagnoses of the problems of Brazil were made and at the end of the program we must create a project that impacts more than 1 million people within one of these diagnoses that was presented to us. Currently we are in the construction of this project and I am focusing on the health area because I believe it is a sector that shouts for help in our country.

I received a proposal to work in São Paulo and I felt it was time to take a step, so I left everything behind and moved to São Paulo where I am having several experiences that prove to me every day that my decision was the correct one. Also selected as Campus Director of the HultPrize Challenge here in São Paulo, so I started to set up a schedule for activities in 2019. This was a bit of my story, if you would like to talk and know more, please let me know. It will be a pleasure to share with you !!
